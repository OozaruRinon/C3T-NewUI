What is this?

C3T is a smart contract which facilitates storage of TRX and distribution of Dividends, as a "simulation" of the cryptocurrency space as a whole.

C3T is based on the concept of several "PoWH" games and Dapps, and is Open Source.

How does it work?

When C3T is purchased, the price of each token rises by a small amount - and when tokens are sold, the price of each token lowers by a small amount. Every token is backed by the TRX stored in the C3T Smart Contract - meaning every token will always have value.

Additionally, every Transfer, Trade, Buy or Sell of C3T has a flat fee of 10%. This fee makes up the TRX which is paid as Dividends to token holders.

How do I make profit?

There are several strategies you could follow - the best one is the one you form for yourself. When you have C3T, you're earning Dividends. Compound those Dividends into more C3T, and get more Dividends faster - or, withdraw those Dividends to your wallet, and HODL onto your existing C3T.

Whatever you decide is up to you - do keep in mind, the market is constantly active, so the price / value could change at any time - it is up to you when you buy or sell C3T. It is for this reason that you should only invest with funds you are willing to lose.

Ready to get started?

You'll need some Tron (TRX) and a compatible wallet, such as TronLink (Google Chrome link here) to use C3T in its complete capacity...
